Why I Started Creating Church Cartoons

July 2026

My faith is an important part of my life, and I wanted to find a creative way to encourage people through visual storytelling.

One project I am working on is creating church-related cartoons that communicate biblical themes in an engaging and accessible format.

Through this project I am learning creative design, storytelling, and how technology can support ministry and community engagement.

This blog will document the lessons, challenges, and progress of that journey.
